Frequently Asked Questions

Can a contested divorce be converted into mutual consent divorce?

Yes. If both parties arrive at a settlement, it may be converted subject to legal requirements and court approval.

Can men claim maintenance?

In appropriate cases, depending on the facts and applicable law, maintenance claims may be considered.

Will I get interim maintenance immediately?

Courts may grant interim maintenance depending on facts, urgency and materials placed before the court.

Can adult children claim maintenance?

Eligibility depends on the applicable law and circumstances such as dependency or inability to maintain themselves.

How quickly can bail be obtained?

It depends on the nature of the offence, facts, court schedule and applicable legal provisions.

What is a Domestic Violence Case?

DVC proceedings may provide protection, residence, monetary relief, custody and compensation depending on facts.

Do you provide online consultation?

Yes. Online consultation is available for clients who want to save time, avoid travel or are outside Hyderabad.
